Go ZipPay


About the project: ZipPay is a Electronic Payment Platform to automate your personalized discounts and coupon codes, on the go, and make you smile as you pay! Building a PWA(Progressive Web Application) by integrating the power of Computational Intelligence with Cloud as a Service through Microsoft Azure and encryption based security to make the complete process safe along with faster transmission speed. Solving the problem of "offers and discounts" which everyone loves but still pay for. Why pay, when you can, Go ZipPay!
